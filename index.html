Things To Do When You're Bored!

For Teens and Tweens

Have your ever been bored out of your mind? Or thought for hours about what you should do? Maybe you've had a little bit of extra time, but couldn't think of anything to do during it? Either way, we've all been bored at some(many)point(s)in our lives, but don't stay that way! Here you will find various things to do when you're bored, so if you're ready to say goodbye to boredom, let's jump in!

What To Do When You're At Home:

Being home alone is the most common way to get bored. You've got no one to talk to, and (if you're on here) nothing to do. But hopefully this list will change that!

  • Try Learning A New Language!

    Learning a new language isn't easy, but it is a nice way to keep youreslf occupied, and could prove useful in the future. Try using an app to get started such as Duolingo or Rossetta Stone.

  • Go On An Online Shopping Spree!

    If, like me, you don't enjoy getting out of the house to shop, but you know you need to, get online! You have something to do, and you get everything you need all from the comfort of your home. So it's definitely a great way to make use of your extra time!

  • Play A Video Game!

    Downloading and playing a new video game, or just play one of your favorites is a great way to pass the time, especially if you've tried all else. The Nintendo Switch is easily a great recomendation, since you can play multiple different games on it, but Xbox and Fortnite are great options too!

  • Watch a movie!

    Imagine watching a moive at home, with a bowl of popcorn and soda, possibly in a nice cozy blanket on your couch, with the lights turned off. Movies are a great form of entertainment, and will certainly lift your bored spirits.

  • Meditate

    Meditating might sound boring, but it helps to get you calmed down and maybe afterwards you'll be able to think of something to do yourself!

  • Organize Something

    Organizing things usually clears a lot of people's minds, so clean your room, rearrange your clothing in color order, create a playlist of your favorite songs.

  • Learn an instrument!

    Music is said to be nectar to the soul. But you don't just have to listen to it, you could play it too. Picking up an instrument is not only a great skill to have, but also full of benefits. It helps you memorize things quicker and work much better in a team, so why not start now?

  • Read!

    Reading is a great way to indulge yourself. Try reading something other than your usual genre, as that can always be insightful.

  • Enroll In A New Class!

    Picking up a new skill isn't a bad idea. Enrolling in a new class might be the best way to connect with other people and make new friends.

  • Bake Something Sweet

    Whenever most people crave a sweet treat, they head to the bakery. You don't have to do that, you can bake your own. Baking something usually makes most people delighted, and it never fails to disapoint with something to eat afterwards!

What To Do When You're Outside

Have you ever been sent outside on account of your staying inside for too long?Well if you have, or if you've stepped outside for a bit of fresh air, you can always check this list to see what you can do!

  • Do Some Gardening

    Plant something in your front or back yard so that you can show off your gardening skills to your friends, or do it to simply make your home look more welcoming. Either way gardening is a fun activity to do outside.

  • Grass Weave

    Grass weaving is where you take a few blades of dry grass, and "knit" them together using your fingers. You can pass hours this way, and you might come out with a nice product too! Learn how to weave a Grass Basket Here.

  • Birdwatch!

    Grab a pair of binoculars and head outside. Birdwatching is a fun way to pass the time outside. As a bonus, record the birds you've found in a birdwatcher book, so you'll always remember them.

  • Stargaze!

    Buy your very own Telescope, head outside on a nice clear night, lay out a blanket, grab some snacks, and sit down to stargaze. Stargazing is a beautiful, calming, and quiet passtime, and who knows? You might even discover a new star!

  • Do Some Yoga

    If you're outside with absolutely nothing else to do, grab a blanket, lay it on the ground, play a tutorial, or some music, and start doing some yoga. Yoga is a calming and fun thing to do, and there are many groups and competitions you can participate in.
